Theo Calder

Theo Calder is a minimalist sculptor known for his geometric designs and use of industrial materials. His work explores the relationship between space, form, and light, creating striking yet simple pieces that evoke a sense of quiet balance.

Galleries

Equilibrium

"Equilibrium" is a minimalist exploration of balance and tension, created from steel and concrete. The sculpture juxtaposes solid and void, with sharp geometric forms intersecting open spaces. Its industrial materials reflect a raw, modern aesthetic, while the play of light and shadow brings dynamic life to its static form. Standing in quiet solitude, "Equilibrium" invites viewers to reflect on the delicate harmony between contrasting forces in space and structure.

Eclipse Bloom

Eclipse Bloom is a mixed-media masterpiece by Lyra Saphire, featuring a swirling, dreamlike garden illuminated by an otherworldly light. The piece combines hand-painted organic patterns with shimmering digital projections, creating a dynamic interplay of shadow and glow. It symbolizes cycles of growth and renewal, capturing the beauty found in the fleeting moments between darkness and light.
